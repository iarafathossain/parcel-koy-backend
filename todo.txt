When you are ready to deploy your backend to Render, here is exactly how you configure Stripe:

Go to the Stripe Dashboard: Log in and make sure you are in the correct mode (Test mode for testing, or Live mode for real money).

Navigate to Webhooks: Go to Developers -> Webhooks.

Add Endpoint: Click the "Add an endpoint" button.

Enter your Render URL: In the "Endpoint URL" box, paste your live Render URL plus the webhook path.
(Example: https://your-app-name.onrender.com/api/v1/webhooks/stripe)

Select Events: Click "Select events", search for checkout.session.completed, and add it.

Save: Click "Add endpoint".
